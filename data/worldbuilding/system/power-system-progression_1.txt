================================================================================
                    POWER SYSTEM — PROGRESSION
                    Spirit Dominion | The System Era
================================================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Every integrated entity in Universe 711 operates on two parallel progression
tracks simultaneously:

  1. CLASS LEVEL   — Skills, combat role, and technique-based advancement.
  2. RACE LEVEL    — Biological and metaphysical sublimation of the body itself.

Both tracks must advance in tandem at critical nodes. Neglecting one limits
the ceiling of the other. Together they define the full scope of a character's
power.

================================================================================
TRACK 1 — CLASS LEVEL
================================================================================

RANK STRUCTURE
--------------
Class progression is divided into ranks, each with a level cap. Moving through
ranks represents mastery deepening and power scaling beyond the previous ceiling.

  Rank F  — Novice         15 levels    Basic quality minimum
  Rank E  — Apprentice     25 levels    Basic quality minimum
  Rank D  — Adept          60 levels    Elite quality minimum
  Rank C  — Elite         100 levels    Elite quality minimum
  Rank B  — Master                      Rare
  Rank A  — Legend                      Rare
  Rank S  — Ascendant                   Epic
  Rank SS — Transcendent                Legendary
  Rank Ex — Supreme                     Celestial

POWER SCALING REFERENCE
-----------------------
A rough real-world equivalent for each rank to anchor the scale of threats
and capabilities:

  Rank F  : Still vulnerable to conventional weapons and small caliber firearms.
  Rank E  : Can engage properly armed soldiers. Resistant to small and large
            caliber firearms.
  Rank D  : Power comparable to conventional war machines. Resistant to most
            kinetic weapons. Begins stepping outside the scope of ordinary life.
  Rank C  : Can survive weapons of mass destruction. Enough power to destroy
            a city.
  Rank B  : Natural disaster level. First major threshold. Abilities begin
            exceeding mortal comprehension. At full power, can destroy a large
            country and permanently alter the environment.
  Rank A  : Free from the constraints of a planet. Walking calamities capable
            of surviving in space. Can destroy an entire world under the right
            conditions. Extremely difficult to kill.
  Rank S+ : Beyond conventional quantification. See Sublimation stages.

CLASS QUALITY
-------------
Every class carries an inherent quality rating that determines the ceiling of
its benefits — AP granted per level, skill quality, and advancement potential.

  Basic / Ordinary   (F tier)
  Elite              (E tier)
  Rare               (D tier)
  Specialist / Epic  (C tier)
  Apex               (B tier)
  Legendary          (A tier)
  Celestial          (S tier)
  Forgotten/Ancient  (SS tier)
  Unique             (Ex tier)

Note: A character's class quality must meet minimum thresholds at certain ranks.
As rank increases, a Basic class may become insufficient — the character either
advances their class or faces a hard ceiling on progression.

CLASS ADVANCEMENT MECHANICS
----------------------------

Leveling up:
  - Performing actions aligned with the class generates class experience.
  - A pure swordsman gains no class EXP from firing a gun.
  - Each level-up increases the class's main attributes automatically and
    awards Free Attribute Points.
  - At certain level thresholds, new skills become available.
  - The quantity of AP, number of skills, and their quality all scale with
    class quality.

Rank-up:
  When the maximum level of a rank is reached, two paths are available:

  Simple Rank-Up:
    The class remains the same but advances to the next rank.
    Example: Rank E Spearman → Rank D Spearman.

    Class quality minimum — HARD BLOCK:
    Certain ranks require a minimum class quality to advance. This is not
    a penalty or a soft cap — it is an absolute System requirement.
    A character whose class quality falls below the minimum for their
    current rank cannot rank up under any circumstance until the class
    quality requirement is met.

    Minimums:
      Rank F → Rank E  : Basic quality minimum
      Rank E → Rank D  : Basic quality minimum
      Rank D → Rank C  : Elite quality minimum
      Rank C → Rank B  : Elite quality minimum

    Practical implication:
    Every character aiming for Rank D must complete a class advancement
    challenge and reach Elite quality first. This is not optional content
    — it is a structural prerequisite. Class advancement challenges become
    mandatory milestones, not optimization choices.

  Class Advancement:
    Requires completing a challenge — the nature of which varies by class
    and rank. Success unlocks an evolved or entirely new class.
    Advancement benefits:
      — Increased attribute conversion ratios
      — Improved attribute distribution
      — Skill points
      — Skill tier advancement
      — For Rare quality and above: special organ strengthening and/or
        knowledge transmission

================================================================================
TRACK 2 — RACE LEVEL  [ SUBLIMATION ]
================================================================================

SUBLIMATION STAGES
------------------
Race progression — also called Sublimation — represents the fundamental
transformation of a being's physical and metaphysical nature. It runs
parallel to class progression and must align with class advancement at
critical breakthrough nodes.

  Stage 1 — Dormant        (Class G equivalent)
  Stage 2 — Awakened       (Class F, E, D)       Mortal tier
  Stage 3 — Extraordinary  (Class C, B)           Super-dimensional tier
  Stage 4 — Named          (Class A, S)           Super-dimensional High tier
  Stage 5 — Eternal        (Class SS)             High-Information tier
  Stage 6 — Primordial     (Class Ex)             Beyond classification

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STAGE 1 — DORMANT
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The default state of any species before contact with supernatural or System
energy. Most beings in Universe 711 were at this stage before the Ascension
event. No access to the extraordinary. Bound entirely by natural biology.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STAGE 2 — AWAKENED  [ Book 1 scope ]
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The first true contact with System power. The character gains access to
extraordinary capabilities but remains fundamentally constrained by their
physical body and its natural laws.

Characteristics:
  — Physiological needs persist (food, sleep, oxygen).
  — Lifespan remains limited.
  — Most magical and extraordinary armament still poses a serious threat.
  — Certain body parts remain critical weak points.
  — Recovery from serious injury is slow.
  — Limited post-action capacity — severe impairment in hostile environments.
  — Elemental access is limited to a single, relatively basic element.
  — Spells and skills are power-reliant rather than refined — quantity
    over quality. Physical skills push the body toward its limits rather
    than transcending them.

This is the entirety of Book 1's scope for Jay and most characters.
The ceiling of what Awakened beings can achieve is vast — F through D rank
covers an enormous range from vulnerable-to-firearms to war-machine-equivalent.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STAGE 3 — EXTRAORDINARY  [ Book 2+ milestone ]
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The first true break from mortal constraints. The character ceases to use
general-purpose mana and gains access to a higher, more refined energy
suited to their specific path. This transition dramatically increases power
and endurance in sustained combat.

Note: Some doors close at this stage. The path chosen shapes what becomes
accessible and what does not.

The super-dimensional designation reflects the character beginning to touch
the fundamental fabric of the world — no longer merely operating within it.

Rank C advancement:
  — Acquisition of advanced energy.
  — Awakening of a new elemental affinity.
  — The character begins forging either a Battle Body (physical path) or
    a True Self (spiritual/mental path) by nourishing it with their
    advanced energy.

Battle Body / True Self:
  A passive effect permanently granted upon completion. Its nature depends
  entirely on the type of energy used to forge it. This is not a technique
  — it is a fundamental transformation of what the character is.

  Upon completion:
  — Attributes are compressed — the same number now represents
    dramatically greater actual power.
  — A Golden Attribute is unlocked.

Golden Attribute:
  A single attribute that amplifies every aspect of the character in its
  direction. Categories: Might / Speed / Psychic / Magic / Resilience / Life.

  Mechanics:
  — Every point of the golden attribute unleashes its full effect without
    physical backlash.
  — The value of 1 Golden Point is determined by the highest value of the
    character's primary attribute at the moment of acquisition.
    Example: If primary attribute is 10 at acquisition, then 1 GP = 10.
  — Golden Attributes can only be obtained through significant achievement
    or high-value System quests. They cannot be farmed.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STAGE 4 — NAMED
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The summit of pure numerical might. To reach Named stage, a character must
accumulate sufficient achievement through the Extraordinary stage and earn
a True Name — the crystallization of everything they have accomplished and
become.

True Name:
  The True Name is not a title assigned by others. It is a name that
  emerges from the weight of a character's existence — their deeds,
  their nature, their path. Its power is proportional to that weight.

  The True Name unlocks:
  — The Avatar / Incarnation: the active form of the Battle Body / True Self.
    At full activation, this represents a nearly limitless release of
    destructive force, drawing from the dimensional energy well directly.
    Duration, power, and manifestation depend on the value of the True Name.
  — The Domain: the projection of the character's will upon the surrounding
    world. Unlike the Avatar which empowers the self, the Domain suppresses
    and affects everything within its reach.

Domain effects (variable by character):
  Elemental suppression / Life drain / Energy drain / Continuous attacks /
  Ruler compulsion / Instant execution / Environmental domination.
  The only counter to a Domain is another Domain.

Named beings in practice:
  Nearly indestructible. Capable of effortlessly destroying worlds if
  unconstrained. The Domain is also the bridge to the next stage — it
  is how a Named being learns to connect to the world at a structural level,
  preparing the path to Eternal.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STAGE 5 — ETERNAL
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
At this stage, raw power is no longer the question. An Eternal being is a
Domain lord — nigh indestructible, capable of living for eons. And yet death
remains a looming possibility even here.

The only path to true Eternal status: becoming part of the universe itself.

THE LAWS
Two categories of Laws coexist and intersect across all universes:

  Physical Laws    : Universal constants governing the material world.
                     Examples: Gravity, Time, Fire, Death, Space.
  Conceptual Laws  : Abstract forces governing existence beyond the physical.
                     Examples: Ambition, Order, Chaos, Will, Memory, Identity.

Each Law contains multiple Concepts within it — the specific principles
that give that Law its shape and expression.
  Example: The Law of Fire contains concepts of Creation, Destruction,
           Hope, Purification, and Consumption.

SEIZING CONCEPTS
An Eternal does not need to master an entire Law — only complete control
over one Concept within a Law is required. The process:

  Step 1 — Perception   : Touch the Law. Perceive its structure and the
                          Concepts contained within it.
  Step 2 — Understanding : Develop deep comprehension of a specific Concept
                          to the point of direct interaction.
  Step 3 — Seizure      : Wrest complete control of the Concept. Inscribe
                          your mark on it — so that as long as the universe
                          persists, you persist within that Concept.

Proof of Eternal status: A being who has seized a Concept can only be
harmed by the Laws. Conventional power cannot permanently destroy them.

MULTIPLE CONCEPTS
An Eternal can seize more than one Concept. Each additional seizure is
exponentially more difficult:
  — The first Concept establishes the foundation.
  — Each subsequent Concept requires greater Domain influence and often
    direct conflict with Eternals holding competing claim.
  — The number of Concepts controlled is the primary measure of power
    and influence at this stage.

CONCEPT ACCUMULATION & PRIMORDIAL PATH
Once a Concept is seized, the Eternal can seek fragments of that same
Concept scattered across different Laws. Collecting enough fragments
allows construction of a Concept Prototype — the final preparation for
Primordial ascension.
  Example: An Eternal who seized Destruction within the Law of Fire can
           seek Destruction fragments within the Laws of Time, Chaos,
           and Death — building toward a complete Prototype of Destruction.

WORLD PRESENCE
Eternal beings operate primarily through Avatars — projected manifestations
of controlled power that can be destroyed without harming the true self.
Their true form exists at a higher plane. Interaction with the world below
happens through Avatars, passive Domain influence, and Concept radiation
that shapes the environment around their territory.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STAGE 6 — PRIMORDIAL
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Beyond conventional classification. The endpoint of the progression path
as currently understood. Details intentionally limited.

RACE & BLOODLINE
================================================================================

RACE CLASSIFICATION
-------------------
Every character belongs to a race — a group of beings sharing core biological
and metaphysical characteristics. Race determines the natural ceiling of
sublimation, the benefits of racial level-up, and base attribute tendencies.

Race tiers by sublimation potential:

  Basic / Mortal          Can reach Awakened naturally.
                          Extraordinary requires external factors (class system).
  Intermediate / Super-dimensional   Can reach Extraordinary naturally.
  Advanced / High-Information        Can reach Named naturally.
  Unique Entity           Classification outside standard tiers.

Each tier can be further divided: Low / Mid / High.

Benefits of racial level-up scale with race rating — higher races grant more
AP, stronger characteristics, and better inherited skills.

Most races are linked to a higher race they can evolve into through sublimation.

INTELLIGENT RACE CATEGORIES
----------------------------
Intelligent races are classified by their civilizational tendency and power
system affinity. In Universe 711's fusion world, humans are not uniform —
they vary across categories depending on their origin world among the five
that merged during Ascension.

  Crafting Race:
    High-intellect races relying on advanced technology and machinery.
    Weakest individually but highest potential for adaptability and
    collective destructive capability. Their creations compensate for
    physical limitations.
    Core attributes : Dexterity, Wisdom, Perception, Willpower.
    Example         : Terran (Human-type I)

  Power Race:
    Physically dominant races relying on raw bodily strength. Technology
    is often backward. Warlike culture with deep respect for power.
    Core attributes : Constitution, Strength, Agility, Willpower.

  Spirit Race:
    Races with an innate connection to nature and the extraordinary.
    Even before System integration, they could access limited natural
    power. Technology is advanced but organic rather than destructive.
    Core attributes : Mystery, Willpower, Perception, Wisdom.
    Note            : Often intermediate races.

  Faith Race:
    Races organized around belief in a higher power. Survival is often
    dependent on a patron being or supernatural force. Society tends to
    be hierarchical around religious leadership. Attributes and capabilities
    vary significantly based on the object of their belief.
    Core attributes : Mystery, Perception.

BLOODLINE
---------
A bloodline is a power specific to a subset within a race — inherited,
passed generation to generation, and representing an edge over racial peers.

Bloodline can translate into:
  — Additional attribute points
  — Inherited Talents
  — Inherited Skills
  — Other unique benefits

Bloodline is the primary power source for monsters and non-intelligent species.
For intelligent beings, it is an add-on layered over racial baseline.

Bloodline quality tiers:
  Common
  Uncommon
  Refined
  Noble
  Sovereign
  Mythic
  Primordial

================================================================================
MONSTER PATH
================================================================================

Monsters progress on a parallel track to intelligent beings. They are the
primary hunting target of the System and the main source of combat experience
and resources for advancing characters.

Key differences from intelligent species:
  — Monsters advance almost exclusively through race level, not class level.
  — At the same rank, monster level caps are 2× those of intelligent species.
  — The primary determinant of a monster's path and ceiling is their bloodline.

Monster rank titles:

  Rank F  — Monster
  Rank E  — Elite Monster
  Rank D  — Tyrant Monster
  Rank C  — Titan / Spirited Monster
  Rank B  — Disaster Monster
  Rank A  — Calamity Monster
  Rank S  — Apocalypse Monster
  Rank SS — Divine Monster
  Rank Ex — Primordial Monster

Promotion paths and sublimation stages mirror those of intelligent species.
Monster bloodline tiers follow the same quality structure as intelligent
bloodlines and are the defining factor in how high a monster can ascend.
