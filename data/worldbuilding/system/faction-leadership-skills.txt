================================================================================
                    FACTION & LEADERSHIP SKILLS
                    Spirit Dominion | The System Era
================================================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Leadership in the System is not an innate attribute. It is not a number
assigned at birth or allocated through Free Attribute Points. It is a
capability earned through the act of leading — recognized by the System
through a dedicated set of skills unlocked by position, achievement, and
faction progression.

A character with no faction has no access to these skills regardless of
their personal power. A character who builds, leads, and grows a faction
unlocks them progressively as their authority and achievement accumulate.

This means leadership is always earned, never assumed. The System reflects
what a leader has actually done — not what they were born with.

Mechanically, leadership skills affect two domains:
  - INTERNAL  : Recruitment, loyalty, retention, faction cohesion.
  - EXTERNAL  : Morale buffs, ally combat enhancement, negotiation.

================================================================================
SKILL TIERS
================================================================================

Skills are grouped into four functional tiers. Unlock conditions are tied
to faction rank, size, and achievement milestones — not personal level.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TIER 1 — RECRUITMENT
"Building the foundation."
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Skills that govern the ability to attract, assess, and bind members to
the faction. Unlocked early — a faction cannot grow without them.

  Faction Call
    Broadcast the faction's presence and intent to unaffiliated individuals
    in range. Increases the likelihood of voluntary recruitment approaches.
    Effect scales with faction rank and reputation.

  Evaluation Eye
    Passive. Grants the leader a basic System-assisted read on a target's
    class, approximate rank, and potential when in direct interaction.
    Does not reveal full stats — only a surface assessment.
    Accuracy improves with skill level.

  Contract Binding
    Formalizes agreements between the faction and new members through the
    System. Bound contracts carry System-enforced consequences for breach.
    Higher skill level allows more complex contract terms.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TIER 2 — LOYALTY
"Keeping what you've built."
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Skills that govern the cohesion, morale, and retention of faction members.
Unlocked as faction size and internal complexity grow.

  Rally
    Active. In critical moments, the leader can issue a System-amplified
    call that stabilizes the mental state of nearby faction members.
    Counters fear, confusion, and morale collapse in combat or crisis.
    Cooldown applies.

  Shared Will
    Passive. Faction members within range of the leader gain a minor
    resistance to mental interference and morale debuffs. Effect radius
    and strength scale with faction rank.

  Oath of the Faction
    A binding ceremony that deepens the System contract between a member
    and the faction. Increases loyalty rating, reduces defection risk,
    and grants minor stat bonuses to the bound member as a System reward
    for commitment. Requires willing participation — cannot be forced.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TIER 3 — COMBAT COMMAND
"Leading from the front or the rear."
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Skills that provide direct combat enhancement to allies and coordinated
tactical effects. Unlocked through combat leadership achievements —
surviving engagements as a faction, not just as an individual.

  Commander's Presence
    Passive. Allies fighting within the leader's awareness range receive
    a baseline combat buff — minor increases to attack, defense, and
    stamina efficiency. Scales with skill level and faction rank.

  Battle Coordination
    Active. The leader designates a tactical formation or target priority
    through the System interface. Participating members gain synchronized
    attack and defense bonuses when operating within the designated
    parameters. Requires members to have Oath of the Faction or equivalent
    binding to activate fully.

  Vanguard Aura
    Active. The leader's presence on the frontline generates an aura of
    pressure that buffs nearby allies and applies a minor suppression
    effect on enemies within range. Effect is stronger the higher the
    leader's personal combat power relative to enemies in range.
    Cannot be faked — the leader must be genuinely in danger for the
    full effect to activate.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TIER 4 — NEGOTIATION & AUTHORITY
"Power expressed without violence."
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Skills unlocked at higher faction ranks. Govern interaction with rival
factions, neutral parties, and System-recognized political structures.

  Iron Terms
    Active. When entering a System-recognized negotiation, the leader
    can lock proposed terms through the interface — making them visible
    to all parties and creating a formal record. Prevents retroactive
    denial of agreed terms. Increases negotiation credibility.

  Threat Assessment
    Passive. In diplomatic or confrontational interactions, the leader
    receives a System-assisted read of the opposing party's intent,
    stress indicators, and approximate faction strength. Not mind-reading
    — a pattern recognition and data synthesis tool.

  Diplomatic Immunity
    Unlocked at advanced faction rank. When operating under a System-
    recognized diplomatic flag, the leader and a designated party gain
    temporary protection from unprovoked System-sanctioned aggression.
    Does not prevent all attacks — removes System legitimacy from those
    who breach it, with corresponding penalties applied by the System.

================================================================================
UNLOCK CONDITIONS — GENERAL FRAMEWORK
================================================================================

  Tier 1 — Recruitment   : Unlocked upon founding a faction and registering
                           it with the System. Available from Arc 1.

  Tier 2 — Loyalty       : Unlocked as faction membership grows past initial
                           threshold and first internal challenges are
                           survived. Mid Arc 1 to Arc 2.

  Tier 3 — Combat Command: Unlocked through combat leadership achievements —
                           leading faction members through significant
                           engagements. Arc 2 onwards.

  Tier 4 — Negotiation   : Unlocked at higher faction rank. Requires the
                           faction to be recognized as a meaningful power
                           by the System. Arc 3 onwards.

================================================================================
DESIGN NOTES
================================================================================

  - These skills reflect what Jay has actually done as a leader, not what
    he was born capable of. The System rewards earned authority.

  - Jay's personal combat power feeds directly into Vanguard Aura — one
    of the few leadership skills where individual strength and leadership
    reinforce each other explicitly.

  - Evaluation Eye is intentionally limited at early levels — Jay will
    get surface reads, not full profiles. Learning to read people beyond
    what the System tells him is a narrative skill, not a mechanical one.

  - Contract Binding and Oath of the Faction create a legal and social
    infrastructure for the faction that becomes politically important
    in later arcs.
