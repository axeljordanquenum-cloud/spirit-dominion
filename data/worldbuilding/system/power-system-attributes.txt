================================================================================
                    POWER SYSTEM — ATTRIBUTES
                    Spirit Dominion | The System Era
================================================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Attributes are the numerical foundation of every entity integrated into the
System. They represent the measurable qualities of a being's physical, mental,
and extraordinary capabilities. They are not merely numbers — each attribute
maps directly to biological and metaphysical components of the body and mind,
and their growth produces real, tangible change in those components.

The System provides a unified rank track across all attribute types. Physical,
magical, and mental attributes scale together — there is no separate progression
path. A character's overall rank reflects the totality of their development.

ATTRIBUTE GROWTH SOURCES
  - Rank-up rewards        : Automatic attribute points granted on leveling.
  - Racial level-up        : Bonus stats tied to species/race progression.
  - Free Attribute Points  : Manually allocated by the character (see below).
  - Skills / Talents       : Some abilities directly modify attributes.
  - Equipment / Items      : External boosts, temporary or permanent.

SOFT CAPS
  Attributes have no hard ceiling per rank tier. However, diminishing returns
  apply as totals rise — each additional point of Free Attribute allocation
  costs progressively more as the attribute grows. This naturally discourages
  pure single-stat stacking and rewards balanced or specialized builds over
  extreme min-maxing. Top performance is heavily reliant on the user. Baseline
  performance is what the numbers guarantee.

CORE ATTRIBUTES

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PHYSICAL ATTRIBUTES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

CONSTITUTION
  The structural integrity of the physical body. How strong, resilient, and
  durable the physical form is — whether it is built of paper, wood, or steel.
  The foundational support for all physical activity.

  Body components : Skin, bones, joints, internal organs, cell membranes.
  Translates into : HP, stamina pool, physical defense, basic resistance.
  Synergises with : Strength, Agility, Vitality.

  Notes: Core attribute of frontline and high-strength physical classes.
  Everything physical is built on top of constitution.

---

VITALITY
  The activity level of the body. How efficiently it generates energy, sustains
  life functions, and recovers from damage and exertion. The source of the
  body's life force and its recovery capability.

  Body components : Heart, lungs, digestive tract, mitochondria, blood,
                    bone marrow.
  Translates into : Stamina pool, HP recovery rate, stamina recovery rate.
  Synergises with : Constitution.

---

STRENGTH
  The ability to exert physical force. Raw muscular power — the foundation of
  damage output and the capacity to wield heavy equipment.

  Body components : Muscles.
  Translates into : Attack damage, equipment handling capability.
  Synergises with : Constitution, Agility.

---

AGILITY
  The character's speed in all its dimensions. Not just movement, but the full
  chain of motion: from the nerve signal to the strike landing.

  Encompasses     : Movement speed, reaction speed, reflex, acceleration,
                    attack speed, precision of motion.
  Body components : Motor nerves, muscles, joints, tendons.
  Translates into : Movement speed, attack speed, attack rate, initial
                    and maximum speed.
  Synergises with : Strength, Constitution.

---

DEXTERITY
  The character's mastery over their own body. Not how fast they move, but
  how precisely and efficiently they move. The attribute of craft, technique,
  and complex execution.

  Encompasses     : Body control, coordination, attack accuracy, evasion rate,
                    terrain adaptability, balance, flexibility.
  Body components : Joints, tendons, muscles, hands.
  Translates into : Accuracy, critical rate, training speed, movement range,
                    turning speed, strength modulation, deceleration control.
  Synergises with : Perception, Agility.

  Notes: Core attribute for lifestyle and professional skills. Essential for
  mastering complex or specialized weapons and high-tier techniques.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
MENTAL / EXTRAORDINARY ATTRIBUTES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

PERCEPTION
  How completely and efficiently the character receives and processes
  information from the world around them. The bridge between raw sense data
  and actionable awareness.

  Encompasses     : Perception range, perception speed, spatial sense, time
                    perception, danger sense, reflex arc, dynamic and static
                    vision, focus, eye-hand coordination, body control.
  Body components : Sense organs, sensory nerves, brain.
  Translates into : Accuracy, attack range, dodge rate, critical rate,
                    environmental awareness.
  Synergises with : Agility, Willpower, Dexterity.

---

MYSTERY  [ also: Intelligence ]
  Affinity with the extraordinary. The character's ability to sense, absorb,
  store, and manipulate energy beyond the physical. The foundation of all
  magical and mana-based capability.

  Encompasses     : Mana capacity, passive mana regeneration, magic damage,
                    mana control, elemental affinity, magic resistance,
                    mana density.
  Body components : Brain, mana core, magic circuit (mana pathways).
  Translates into : MP total, magic damage, magic resistance, mana regen.
  Synergises with : Willpower, Constitution, Wisdom.

---

WILLPOWER  [ also: Mind ]
  The strength of the mind as a casting and control instrument. Not
  intelligence — this is the raw force of mental will applied to the
  extraordinary. The attribute of execution, not knowledge.

  Encompasses     : Spell control, casting speed, spell learning requirement,
                    spell complexity capacity, casting frequency, active mana
                    regeneration, mental interference resistance, psychic
                    power, perception enhancement.
  Body components : Mind.
  Translates into : Casting speed, spell learning ability, mana regeneration,
                    cooldown reduction, mental resistance.
  Synergises with : Mystery, Wisdom.

---

WISDOM
  Mental prowess and cognitive depth. The ability to process complex
  information, retain knowledge, and apply it with precision. Where Willpower
  is force, Wisdom is finesse.

  Encompasses     : Learning capacity, memory, comprehension, error reduction
                    in spell execution, spell requirement thresholds, spell
                    library capacity.
  Body components : Brain.
  Translates into : Spell fault reduction, spell learning thresholds,
                    knowledge retention, number of learnable spells.
  Synergises with : Perception, Mystery, Constitution.

DERIVED STATS
================================================================================

HEALTH POINTS  [ HP ]
  The character's overall physical state expressed as a single value.
  A reference number — not the full picture of combat viability.

  Formula         : HP = Constitution × 10
  Additional gain : +10 HP per racial level-up (base human rate).

  Important nuance:
  HP is a general indicator. It does not reflect the impact of specific
  injuries. The type and location of a wound must be considered separately
  to assess real combat impact.

  Threshold effects:
  When HP falls below critical thresholds, systemic debuffs apply depending
  on the character's resilience. Example baseline:
    — HP < 50% : All attributes reduced by 10%, stamina depletion accelerated.
  Exact thresholds and debuff severity scale with character resilience and
  relevant skills or talents.

  HP Regeneration:
  Driven by Vitality. Passive regen operates differently in and out of combat.
  Base passive regen is modest — meaningful improvement requires abilities,
  skills, or talents that specifically enhance it.
  Passive regen consumes additional stamina while active.
  Passive regen only functions within a limited HP loss range. Severe injuries
  (limb loss, massive blood loss, deep organ damage) cannot be addressed by
  passive regen — active healing or recovery resources are required.

---

STAMINA
  Physical energy. The resource consumed by all physical actions. The intensity
  of the action determines the consumption rate.

  Formula         : Stamina = Average of (Constitution + Vitality)
  Additional gain : Can be increased through racial level-up.

  Low HP interaction:
  Reduced HP lowers the stamina ceiling and accelerates depletion rate.
  Fighting at critical HP is a compounding disadvantage.

  Stamina Regeneration:
  Requires rest as baseline. Food and special resources can accelerate
  recovery. Standard regeneration rate is governed by Vitality.

---

MANA POINTS  [ MP ]
  The reservoir of magical energy. Fuel for all interaction with extraordinary
  forces. The foundational resource for spirit abilities, spells, and energy-
  based skills.

  Formula         : MP = Mystery × [ conversion rate TBD ]

  Regeneration:
  Passive regen operates continuously in and out of combat. Active regen
  through meditation or consumption of special resources. Regen rate is
  enhanced in energy-rich environments.

  High-cost casting risks:
  Mobilizing large amounts of MP in a short period imposes physical and mental
  strain. Adverse effects scale with the ratio of MP used versus the
  character's Willpower and physical resilience:
    — Dizziness, bleeding, confusion, disorientation.
  Continuous high-cost casting compounds these effects rapidly.

  CRITICAL: Empty MP can result in death. Characters must exercise caution
  when casting expensive abilities in sequence. The burden placed on the mind
  by severe MP depletion is not merely debilitating — it is potentially fatal.

================================================================================
FREE ATTRIBUTE POINTS  [ FA ]
================================================================================

Free Attribute Points are awarded through leveling and can be manually
allocated by the character to any attribute of their choosing. They represent
the player's direct control over their development path.

ALLOCATION MECHANICS

  Delayed absorption:
  Allocated attributes do not take full effect immediately. The body and mind
  require time to integrate the change. Effect builds progressively after
  allocation.

  Regional weighting:
  Allocation affects the whole body, but the intensity of the effect is
  greater in the parts of the body most actively used after allocation.
  Example: A swordsman who allocates Strength points then trains sword work
  will see greater upper-limb strength gains than lower-limb gains in that
  period. Deliberate training focus shapes where the attribute lands hardest.

  Diminishing returns:
  As total attribute values rise, the FA cost to increase them further grows.
  Early points in an attribute are efficient. Later points require
  proportionally more FA for the same numerical gain. This is a soft system —
  there is no hard wall, only increasing cost.

  Human baseline FA per level (pre-Sublimation): 7–10 points.
  Exact amount varies by individual potential.

================================================================================
EXPANDABLE SYSTEM
================================================================================

The twelve core attributes listed above are the standard framework provided
by the System to all integrated entities in Universe 711 at the point of
Ascension. They are not the totality of what is possible.

Rare, special, or exceptional attributes can exist beyond this list. These
may be:
  - Unlocked through exceptional talent or bloodline
  - Granted by unique skills, evolutions, or titles
  - Discovered through contact with rare phenomena or entities
  - Native to civilizations or species outside Universe 711

The existence of attributes beyond the standard list is not common knowledge
at the start of Arc 1. Their discovery will occur naturally through progression.
