RANK DESCRIPTIONS
================================================================================

  RANK F — NOVICE / TRAINEE
  -------------------------
  Individuals at this rank are beginners just starting to develop their
  capabilities. Minimal experience, minimal proficiency. The System has
  recognized their potential but they have yet to demonstrate meaningful
  mastery of any skill or discipline. Most survivors of the Ascension
  event start here.

  RANK E — APPRENTICE
  --------------------
  Apprentices have moved beyond raw beginnings and are actively developing
  their skills through practice and experience. They are gaining real
  competence in their chosen path and beginning to understand the deeper
  mechanics of their class. A Rank E fighter is no longer simply reacting
  — they are learning to think within their discipline.

  RANK D — ADEPT
  ---------------
  Adepts have achieved genuine proficiency. They perform with skill and
  efficiency, demonstrate clear mastery of core techniques, and are
  capable of handling serious threats. At this rank, individuals begin
  stepping outside the scope of ordinary life — their capabilities start
  to exceed what conventional weapons and soldiers can reliably counter.
  Reaching Rank D requires an Elite quality class minimum.

  RANK C — ELITE
  ---------------
  Elites are among the best in their field. Superior skills, deep knowledge,
  and refined capability set them apart from the vast majority of practitioners.
  At this rank, characters can survive weapons of mass destruction and carry
  enough destructive potential to level a city. Notable achievement and
  reputation typically accompany Rank C advancement.

  RANK B — MASTER
  ----------------
  Masters have achieved a level of mastery that places them as authorities
  within their domain. Their abilities surpass most practitioners decisively.
  At this rank, power begins exceeding mortal comprehension — a Master
  operating at full strength can destroy a large country and permanently
  alter the surrounding environment. Natural disaster equivalence.

  RANK A — LEGEND
  ----------------
  Legends are renowned figures whose accomplishments and power are without
  parallel at the mortal level. They have transcended planetary constraints
  — capable of surviving in space, operating freely outside of any single
  world's scope, and destroying entire worlds under the right conditions.
  An A rank individual is extremely difficult to kill by any conventional
  means.

  RANK S — ASCENDANT
  -------------------
  Ascendants have transcended the conventional limits that define even
  Legends. They possess rare or unique capabilities that set them entirely
  apart. At this rank, power can no longer be meaningfully quantified by
  mortal standards. Entry into the Eternal sublimation path.

  RANK SS — TRANSCENDENT
  -----------------------
  Transcendents stand at the pinnacle of skill and existence, surpassing
  even the most legendary figures in history. They are among the greatest
  practitioners across any era. Their mastery is effectively without ceiling
  within the known framework of the System. Deep Law interaction begins here.

  RANK Ex — SUPREME
  ------------------
  Supreme beings are incomparable. They represent the absolute peak of
  power, knowledge, and influence within their domain. Their existence
  alone shapes the world around them. Primordial classification.

================================================================================
CLASS QUALITY TIERS
================================================================================

  BASIC / ORDINARY
  -----------------
  Entry-level classes suitable for those beginning their System journey.
  Straightforward in function, limited in ceiling. A solid starting point
  but insufficient for advancement past the early ranks without upgrading.
  The most common class quality among newly awakened individuals.

  Example — Warrior Apprentice:
    A novice warrior learning the basics of combat with sword and shield.
    Simple abilities: basic attacks, defensive stance, standard strikes.

  ---

  ELITE
  ------
  Strong and reliable classes that perform well across most situations.
  They possess formidable abilities and contribute meaningfully at their
  rank. Popular among serious practitioners seeking a balance of power
  and reliability. Required minimum quality for advancement to Rank D.

  ---

  RARE
  -----
  Less common classes with unique abilities or attributes that differentiate
  them from standard options. They offer specialized gameplay and experiences
  that require more effort or resources to unlock and master. Required
  minimum quality for advancement to Rank C.

  ---

  SPECIALIST / EPIC
  ------------------
  Classes that excel in specific roles or niches, providing unique advantages
  and strategies. Not always the most versatile, but devastating in the right
  context and in skilled hands. Their specialization demands coordination
  and deep understanding to maximize.

  Example — Shadow Assassin:
    A stealthy rogue specializing in ambush and assassination.
    Abilities: Backstab for concentrated burst damage, Vanish to
    disengage and reposition unseen.

  ---

  APEX
  -----
  The pinnacle of power and versatility among accessible classes. Apex
  classes excel across all aspects of engagement and are highly sought
  after for their unparalleled strength and adaptability. Considered the
  strongest reliable choice for consistent high-level performance.

  Example — Divine Champion:
    A chosen warrior blessed with divine authority.
    Abilities: Holy Smite to strike with righteous force,
    Divine Shield to protect allies from harm.

  ---

  LEGENDARY
  ----------
  Classes legendary in nature, possessing unmatched power and abilities
  within their tier. Revered as the epitome of class mastery. Their
  capabilities set them apart from all others at the same rank.

  Example — Archmage of the Arcane Order:
    Master of all schools of magic.
    Abilities: Time Warp to manipulate temporal flow,
    Meteor Storm to rain concentrated destruction.

  ---

  CELESTIAL
  ----------
  Classes imbued with a quality of power that transcends conventional
  classification. Often associated with divine or extraordinary origin.
  They wield capabilities that operate beyond the standard framework
  of the System's lower tiers.

  Example — Avatar of Light:
    An embodiment of celestial purity.
    Abilities: Celestial Nova — blinding explosion of concentrated light,
    Celestial Blessing — healing and protection of allies.

  ---

  FORGOTTEN / ANCIENT
  --------------------
  Ancient or obscure classes lost to time or overlooked by most. They
  may possess hidden potential and unique abilities that are not
  immediately apparent — and are often underestimated for exactly
  that reason. Mastering a Forgotten class requires significant research
  and unconventional thinking.

  Example — Shadowblade Revenant:
    A warrior returned from darkness, wielding shadow powers.
    Abilities: Shadowstep — teleportation through darkness,
    Soul Drain — siphon life force from enemies.

  ---

  UNIQUE
  -------
  One-of-a-kind classes that defy categorization entirely. They offer
  experiences and mechanics unlike any other class — original, unconventional,
  and impossible to fully predict or counter through standard means.
  Unique classes are singular. No two individuals can hold the same one.

  Example — Chronomancer:
    A master of time magic bending the fabric of reality.
    Abilities: Temporal Distortion — slow localized time,
    Rewind — undo recent events and restore a prior state.

SPECIALIST CLASSES — ADDITIONAL EXAMPLES
================================================================================

  Arcane Artificer  [ Rare ]
    A master craftsman who infuses weapons and armor with magical properties.
    Abilities: Arcane Infusion — empower equipment with magical force,
    Construct Summoning — deploy crafted constructs as combat allies.

  Battle-Mage  [ Elite ]
    A spellcaster equally capable in melee and magic.
    Abilities: Arcane Strike — imbue weapons with magical force,
    Shield Barrier — deploy a protective magical field.

NOTES
================================================================================

  - Class quality determines the ceiling of benefits per level: AP granted,
    skill quality, and advancement potential all scale with class quality.

  - A character's class quality must meet rank minimums or advancement is
    hard-blocked by the System. No workarounds exist.

  - Forgotten and Unique classes are intentionally rare in Universe 711
    at the start of Book 1. Knowledge of their existence is limited.
