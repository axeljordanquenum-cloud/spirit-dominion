================================================================================
                    JAY — CLASS STRUCTURE & SKILL REFERENCE
                    Spirit Dominion | The System Era
================================================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Jay begins the story classless. His class is not assigned at Ascension — it
is earned through a time-attack quest issued by the System. His window is
1 hour. The standard window is 24 hours.

The compressed window is not a penalty. It is the System acknowledging his
ceiling is different and demanding he prove it immediately.

Class reward is determined by two factors:
  — Completion speed within the window
  — Achievement quality during the window (kills, survival, actions taken)

Jay earns a high-tier fighter class through performance. The System then
reads his Primal Spirit Orb talent and synthesizes the result — producing
Spirit Fighter as the intersection of what he did and what he is. A fighter
with identical performance but no Primal Spirit Orb would receive a different
class entirely.

================================================================================
CLASS IDENTITY
================================================================================

  Class name    : Spirit Fighter
  Quality       : Elite
  Starting rank : F
  Concept       : Platform class — not a damage class. Provides infrastructure
                  to make Jay's spirits more accessible, more efficient, and
                  more deeply integrated with his combat.

  Core principle:
  Jay channels power through himself. He is not a summoner. Spirits do not
  fight independently at a distance while he stands back. They integrate with
  his body, augment his capabilities, and shape what he is capable of in a
  given fight. The fusion system is the primary expression of this.

AUTO-ATTRIBUTE DISTRIBUTION (class track, ~70% primary / 30% secondary)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Agility        ~28%   Primary physical axis
  Constitution   ~23%   Primary physical axis
  Strength       ~18%   Primary physical axis
  Willpower      ~21%   Spirit control secondary
  Perception     ~10%   Minor spread

  Note: Distribution shifts when a fusion stage is active. Spirit Synergy
  passively transfers 50% of the active spirit's primary attribute and 30%
  of its secondary to Jay — the active spirit shapes the combat ceiling.

================================================================================
CLASS PROGRESSION TRACK
================================================================================

  Each rank-up via the Primal Spirit Orb grants Jay one new spirit.
  Spirits are born from Jay's mental and emotional state at the moment of
  rank-up. The first spirit has a predatory nature — a fragment of something
  primordial that recognized its own nature in Jay.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  RANK F — SPIRIT FIGHTER  |  Concept: Channeling
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  1 spirit. No physical form — cannot be summoned outside Jay's body. Power
  is borrowed. The spirit responds, Jay channels. He must use his single
  spirit intelligently — when to activate, when to hold back, how to make one
  source of power cover every situation. The limitation is the design.

  Gate condition (Lv15): Over-rank OR over-quality kill required.
  Non-qualifying kills yield 5% EXP during gate level.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  RANK E — SPIRIT PREDATOR  |  Concept: Fusion
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  2 spirits. No synergy between them. The challenge shifts — not how to use
  one spirit well, but when to use which spirit and how to transition without
  losing momentum. The boundary between Jay and his first spirit blurs
  permanently. His senses, reflexes, and instincts are no longer purely
  human-baseline.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  RANK D — SPIRIT HERALD  |  Concept: Resonance
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  3 spirits. Jay can now project spirits outside his body as visible
  presences — manifestations, not summons. He gains access to the spirit
  world and begins to understand its structure. From here he hunts other
  spirits to feed his own via Spirit Devouring. His nature begins broadcasting
  outward — other spirits, beasts, and spirit users feel him.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  RANK C — SPIRIT ASCENDANT  |  Concept: Transcendence
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  4 spirits. Jay can evolve his spirits, build physical bodies for them, and
  summon them to act independently. He has an avatar in the spirit world.
  Power extends beyond himself for the first time: Spirit Call temporarily
  draws an unbound spirit and can gift it to someone; Spirit Transfer
  temporarily lends a bonded spirit to an ally. He becomes a node.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  RANK B — SPIRIT EMPEROR  |  Concept: Authority
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  5 spirits. Jay can fuse multiple spirits together temporarily into a single
  unified entity. Spirit Conversion enters the kit — non-spirit matter can be
  turned into spirits. Wild spirits, enemy spirits, and bonded spirits of
  other fighters destabilize in his presence.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  RANK A — SOVEREIGN  |  Concept: Natural Law
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  6 spirits — the sixth is the True Name itself, crystallized into a living
  thing Jay can wield. His spirits become armaments: each takes the form of
  a weapon or armor Jay equips, giving him their full power while wielding
  them. True Name, Avatar, and Domain all active.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  RANK S — PRIMAL INCARNATION  |  Concept: Embodiment
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  7 spirits — the seventh is the Primal Spirit Orb itself. The Talent that
  started everything becomes the final bond. Every spirit is an extension of
  Jay's will. He can act through any of them simultaneously, in any form, with
  full intent. He doesn't cast — he extends.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  RANK SS — ORIGIN SPIRIT  |  Concept: Source
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  All spirits and Jay become one. The Primal Spirit Orb was always a fragment
  of something primordial that recognized its own nature in Jay. At SS, the
  recognition is complete. Every rank was not a becoming — it was the world
  catching up to what was already there.

CONCEPTUAL SPINE
----------------
  Channeling → Fusion → Resonance → Transcendence →
  Authority → Natural Law → Embodiment → Source

================================================================================
SPIRIT FUSION SYSTEM
================================================================================

Each bonded spirit has four independent fusion stages. Stages unlock based
on bond level, skill mastery, and attribute requirements. Higher stages
drain spirit energy continuously — hold duration is limited.

  STAGE 1 — DEMI  (Partial integration)
  ----------------------------------------
  Jay takes on some characteristics of the spirit — subtle physical markers,
  enhanced senses. Bonus attributes from Spirit Synergy amplified.
  Skills: abilities that don't require morphological features or elemental
  affinity. Moderate entry cost, low continuous drain. Extended hold viable.

  STAGE 2 — WERE / WILD  (Significant transformation)
  -----------------------------------------------------
  Body changes substantially — proportions shift, new features emerge.
  Elemental access opens for the first time. Skills requiring the spirit's
  physical affinity become available. High entry cost, meaningful drain.
  Hold duration measured in minutes. Transformation is visible — strategic
  concealment compromised.

  STAGE 3 — MORPHED  (Full transformation)
  -----------------------------------------
  Complete transformation into the spirit's form. Full access to the spirit's
  power. Very high entry cost, severe continuous drain. Hold duration extremely
  limited — decisive intervention window, not sustained combat mode. Human
  cognition partially suppressed; spirit nature bleeds into decision-making.

  STAGE 4 — PERFECT  (Full absorption, no visible change)
  --------------------------------------------------------
  Jay looks entirely human. Spirit's power fully absorbed and expressed
  through human form. All skills human-adapted, no morphological requirements.
  Highest entry cost but paradoxically lower continuous drain than Morphed —
  integration is so complete it is more efficient. Hardest to achieve.
  Requires deep bond level, high skill mastery, significant attribute
  thresholds.

================================================================================
SKILLS — FULL REFERENCE
================================================================================

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RANK F STARTERS  (awarded at class obtention, Race Lv10)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  SPIRIT AFFINITY  [Passive]
  Reduces the base cost of maintaining any active spirit bond. The foundation
  that makes sustained fusion viable. Stacks with Spirit Bond when unlocked.

  SPIRIT LANGUAGE  [Passive]
  Grants intuitive understanding of a bonded spirit's intent, mood, and
  nature. Not verbal — a direct sense of meaning. Deepens as bond level
  grows. Prerequisite for advanced spirit coordination.

  SPIRIT BOND  [Active]
  Deliberately deepens the bond relationship with a target spirit. Accelerates
  bond level growth beyond passive accumulation. Higher bond level unlocks
  deeper fusion stages and stronger Spirit Synergy transfer.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RANK F THRESHOLD UNLOCKS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  SPIRIT SENSE  [Passive]
  Environmental awareness of nearby spirit-type entities — presence, rough
  nature, approximate strength. Not precise detection. Grows more accurate
  with skill level. Also reads a spirit's nature on direct contact.

  SPIRIT FUSION  [Active]
  Initiates a fusion stage with a bonded spirit (Demi, Were, Morphed, or
  Perfect depending on bond depth and requirements). Each stage has an energy
  cost to enter and drains continuously while held. Higher stages drain faster.

  SPIRIT NURTURING  [Passive]
  Accelerates a bonded spirit's natural growth over time. Reduces experience
  and bond depth required for a spirit to evolve. Long-arc skill — cumulative
  effect is most visible across ranks.

  ADAPTIVE PHYSIONOMY  [Passive]
  Jay's body gradually adapts to the physical changes imposed by fusion stages
  — reducing recovery time after exiting a stage and improving tolerance for
  higher-stage transformations over time.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RANK E — NEW UNLOCKS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  SPIRIT SWITCH  [Active]
  Instantly swaps which designated spirit is active mid-combat. Eliminates
  transition lag between spirits. Cost scales with how deep a fusion stage
  Jay is exiting when switching.

  OVERCLOCK  [Active]
  Temporarily pushes Jay's own attributes beyond their current cap by drawing
  on accumulated spirit energy. Short duration, significant backlash if
  overextended. Independent of any fusion stage — works with or without an
  active spirit.

  TWIN STAR DANCE  [Active]
  A coordinated strike technique using two spirits simultaneously.
  Coordination is Jay's doing — the spirits do not cooperate naturally. High
  execution cost. Effective precisely because it appears impossible given the
  lack of spirit synergy at this stage.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RANK D — NEW UNLOCKS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  SPIRIT MANIFESTATION  [Active]
  Projects a bonded spirit as a visible external presence — a projection in
  the physical world. Not a summon; the spirit has no independent body or
  action. Used for intimidation, environmental effect, or extending Jay's
  sensory range.

  SPIRIT JUMP  [Active]
  Teleportation through the spirit world. Jay dissolves into the spirit layer,
  travels instantaneously, and re-emerges at a destination. Range and
  precision scale with spirit world familiarity. Cannot be tracked through
  conventional means during transit.

  SPIRIT DEVOURING  [Active]
  Sacrifices a bonded or captured spirit to dramatically accelerate another
  bonded spirit's growth. Irreversible. The devoured spirit ceases to exist.
  The weight of the decision is built into the mechanic — these are living
  bonds.

  OVERLIMIT BREAK  [Active]
  Pushes a fusion stage beyond its safe threshold — accessing power above what
  the current bond level should allow. Short window. Significant physical
  backlash on exit. Distinct from Overclock: Overlimit breaks the fusion
  ceiling, Overclock breaks the attribute ceiling.

  SPIRIT STRENGTHENING  [Active]
  Channels spirit energy into a bonded spirit to raise its base power level
  permanently over repeated use. Slow but cumulative — the ceiling of what a
  spirit can become is directly influenced by how much Strengthening has been
  applied.

  SPIRIT ENHANCEMENT  [Active]
  Temporarily amplifies Jay's attributes in the direction of a spirit's nature
  for the duration of a fight or engagement. Costs spirit energy. Does not
  require an active fusion stage.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RANK C — NEW UNLOCKS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  SPIRIT SUMMONING  [Active]
  Calls a bonded spirit into the physical world with an independent body to
  act autonomously. Requires a body to have been built for the spirit
  beforehand. The spirit can fight, scout, or act separately from Jay. Costs
  spirit energy proportional to duration.

  SPIRIT ASCENSION  [Active]
  Evolves a bonded spirit to its next form — a permanent transformation that
  raises its power ceiling, unlocks new capabilities, and deepens its nature.
  Requires sufficient bond level, Spirit Nurturing investment, and Spirit
  Strengthening history. Cannot be reversed.

  SPIRIT CALL  [Active]
  Temporarily attracts an unbound spirit from the surrounding environment. The
  spirit responds for a limited duration. Secondary function: Jay can grant
  the attracted spirit as a temporary bond to another person — the only way
  someone without a Talent can access a spirit bond.

  SPIRIT TRANSFER  [Active]
  Temporarily transfers the bond of one of Jay's spirits to another person.
  That person gains access to the spirit's power for the duration. Jay loses
  access to that spirit while the transfer is active. Bond returns
  automatically when the transfer ends or Jay recalls it.

  AVATAR  [Active]
  Projects a presence into the spirit world through which Jay can act,
  perceive, and interact — while his physical body remains in the material
  world. Allows simultaneous existence across both layers.

  SUBJUGATION  [Active]
  Forces an unbound or weakened spirit under Jay's authority. Subjugated
  spirits are not bonded — they are controlled. Control maintained through
  continuous will. Used for hunting, intelligence gathering, or temporary
  combat deployment. A spirit under Subjugation can be Devoured.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RANK B — NEW UNLOCKS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  SPIRIT MERGE  [Active]
  Temporarily fuses two or more of Jay's bonded spirits into a single unified
  entity. The merged spirit inherits combined power and nature from all
  participants. Duration limited. When the merge ends, constituent spirits
  return to their individual states.

  SPIRIT CONVERSION  [Active]
  Converts non-spirit matter — objects, energy, in extreme cases concepts —
  into spirits. The resulting spirit reflects the nature of what was converted.
  Costs proportional to complexity and scale of what is being converted.

  MIRROR BREAK  [Active]
  Overlays a spirit onto Jay that mirrors his own abilities back — a living
  copy of his capability expressed through the spirit's nature. Used
  offensively as a decoy layer, defensively to reflect attacks, or to run
  the same technique from two vectors simultaneously.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RANK A — NEW UNLOCKS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  SPIRIT ARMAMENT  [Active]
  A bonded spirit takes the physical form of a weapon or armor that Jay
  equips. While wielding the armament Jay accesses the spirit's full power
  simultaneously. The form reflects the spirit's nature. The sixth spirit —
  the True Name — is inseparable from this skill's highest expression.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RANK S — EXPRESSIONS (not conventional skill unlocks)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Full spirit extension — every spirit acts as an extension of Jay's will
  simultaneously. Act through any, in any form, with full intent.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RANK SS — EXPRESSIONS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  PRIMORDIAL ECHO  — All previous skills across every rank activate
                     simultaneously as a single unified expression.
  FIRST LAW        — Imposes a rule that predates the System. Cannot be
                     negated because it existed before negation was possible.
  ORIGIN PULSE     — Rewrites the spirit nature of everything in range back
                     to its most fundamental predatory state — civilization,
                     technique, and refinement stripped away.
