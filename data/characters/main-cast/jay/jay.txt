[ PERSONALITY ]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Core Traits     : Calm under pressure. Adaptive. Observant. Pragmatic.
                  Quietly competitive — the ambition runs deep but rarely
                  surfaces as emotion.

Strengths       : Reads situations faster than most. Doesn't panic.
                  Makes decisions without needing certainty.
                  Leads without needing to be liked.
                  Finds the angle others miss because he's already stopped
                  reacting and started thinking.

Flaws           : Emotional distance — connection costs him effort that
                  comes naturally to others.
                  Tendency to carry everything alone longer than necessary.
                  His father's voice lives in him. He doesn't always know
                  when he's following it.

Fears           : Purposeless strength — becoming powerful with nothing
                  behind it.
                  Losing Samantha or Jess.
                  Becoming his father without realizing it.

Motivations     : Reach the top. Not for recognition — because anything
                  less feels like failure. This was installed by Alan and
                  Jay has never fully examined whether it is truly his own.
                  Protect the people he has chosen to keep close.

Moral Alignment : Pragmatic with a private code. Will do what is necessary.
                  Will not harm deliberately without purpose.
                  Will not move against someone weaker than him.
                  These lines are non-negotiable and have never been
                  announced to anyone.

Speech Style    : Sparse. Direct. No performance. Says the necessary thing
                  and stops. In rare moments of ease — warmer, dryer humor
                  surfaces briefly.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ BACKSTORY ]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Origin          : Born into a world of violence he didn't choose. His father
                  Alan ran something dangerous — the kind of household that
                  gifted a puppy one month and a gun the next. His mother
                  was a counterweight who couldn't fully protect him from
                  what Alan decided to make him.

Key Events      : — Rocky. The first lesson. Weakness is an eternal sin.
                  — Samantha. The execution ground where he met her gaze
                    and chose differently than he was instructed.
                  — Years of spartan training under Alan that built the
                    body, the discipline, and the philosophy that now runs
                    him.
                  — The Ascension. Universe 711 integrated. Jay exhaled
                    and said: "So, this is it." He was not surprised.
                    Some part of him had been waiting.

Trauma/Wound    : His father didn't break him — which is almost worse.
                  Alan's worldview took root because it worked. Jay is
                  living proof of his father's method, which makes it
                  very hard to reject entirely.

Secret(s)       : The moral line he holds — not harming the weak, not
                  acting without purpose — is a quiet, private rebellion
                  against Alan that he has never named out loud, even
                  to himself.
