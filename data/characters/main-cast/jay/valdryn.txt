                    SPIRIT PROFILE — VALDRYN
                    Jay's First Spirit | Rank 1 | Primal Spirit Orb

CLASSIFICATION  : Beast Spirit / Moon-Attuned
TYPE            : Dire Wolf Variant
ORIGIN          : Hyper-Dimensional Spirit World
BOND HOST       : Jay

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
APPEARANCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Valdryn is a dire wolf variant — larger and heavier than any natural wolf,
built for lethal efficiency rather than endurance. Its defining features set
it apart from standard dire wolf spirits immediately:

  - Coat         : Abundant silver fur, dense and close to the body.
  - Scales       : Dragon-like scales line the belly and wrap around the
                   limbs. They carry a faint silver-blue iridescence in
                   moonlight or low light conditions.
  - Eyes         : Moon-attuned — pale silver or white, with a faint inner
                   luminescence when its abilities are active.
  - Build        : Lean through the haunches, heavily muscled in the
                   shoulders and forelimbs. The claws are disproportionately
                   long and razor-defined — the primary weapon.
  - Presence     : Even at its weakest, newborn state, the Valdryn carries
                   a weight to it. Something in its bearing that other
                   creatures register before it moves.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
NATURE & LORE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Valdryn is a solitary creature by nature. Unlike most wolf spirits, it
does not operate within a pack structure — and when it does exist alongside
others, it can only occupy one role: alpha. Subordination is not in its
architecture.

This mirrors Jay's own disposition — not anti-social, but fundamentally
incapable of following someone else's lead once his own judgment is engaged.
The Valdryn is not a coincidence. It is a reflection.

Moon attunement grants the Valdryn a connection to cycles, timing, and
precision. Its power is not brute force — it is the right strike, at the
right moment, delivered with lethal accuracy. Its scaling into prolonged
engagements through its skillset allows it to outlast opponents who
expect it to burn out quickly.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
COMBAT PROFILE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Specialty     : Fast, precise assault — leading with claws, exploiting
                openings, building pressure through mobility and aggression.

Secondary     : Sustained engagement — selected skills allow the Valdryn
                to remain dangerous in extended fights, not just burst windows.

Weakness      : Early stage, the spirit is newborn and raw. Its power ceiling
                is visible but distant. Jay must grow it deliberately.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SKILLSET — FULL POOL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Skills are unlocked progressively as the Valdryn grows through its bond
with Jay. The starting kit reflects the spirit's newborn state — functional
but limited. The full pool represents its potential across the arc of the story.

  [ ARC 1 — AVAILABLE AT START ]

  Silver Assault
    A precise, accelerated claw strike enhanced by the Valdryn's moon
    attunement. Delivers concentrated force to a single point. Effective
    against armored or scaled targets. The silver quality of the strike
    carries additional effect against certain creature types.

  Phantom Dance
    A short-burst movement ability. The Valdryn — or Jay channeling it —
    shifts position with near-instant speed, leaving a faint afterimage.
    Used offensively to close distance or create angles. Used defensively
    to break out of enclosed pressure. Not infinite — costs energy per use.

  Blood Feast
    On landing a damaging strike, the Valdryn partially recovers energy
    from the wound. Sustain mechanic — keeps the spirit viable in prolonged
    exchanges without needing to disengage. Scales with damage dealt.

  Resilient Will
    Passive. When the Valdryn or Jay would be pushed past a threshold of
    damage, Resilient Will triggers a brief resistance state — reducing
    incoming damage and steadying the bond. Does not prevent damage entirely.
    Acts as a floor, not a shield.

SPIRIT SYNERGY CONTRIBUTION — ARC 1
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Per the Primal Spirit Orb's Spirit Synergy passive, Jay passively receives
from the Valdryn at Arc 1:

  - 50% of Valdryn's primary attribute     : Agility
  - 30% of Valdryn's secondary attribute   : Strength
  - 2 random skills from starting pool     : [ TBD — assign on rank-up ]

  Note: Spirit is the Valdryn's tertiary attribute. It contributes to the
  bond's sustainability and evolution trajectory but is not part of the
  Spirit Synergy passive split at this stage.

As the Valdryn grows, these passive contributions scale accordingly.

                   have that knowledge at Arc 1.
  Growth driver  : Combat, bond deepening, Origin Infusion (passive).
  Evolution path : [ TBD — to be defined as story progresses ]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
NARRATIVE ROLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Valdryn is Jay's first reflection. Born at the moment of Ascension, it
embodies who Jay is at that exact instant — the solitary predator, the
precise and controlled aggressor, the creature that cannot follow but
has not yet fully become the thing that leads.

As Jay changes, the Valdryn does not change its nature. But it grows into
more of what it already is. The question the spirit quietly poses across
Book 1 is whether Jay's solitary-alpha nature is a strength, a limitation,
or both.
