Core Traits     : Methodical and precise. She approaches every problem
                  the way she approaches a construction: understand
                  the load, identify the structure, build to last.
                  Quietly formidable. Does not announce herself.
                  Deeply loyal to the people she has chosen.

Strengths       : Mastery of complexity without needing to simplify
                  it first. Where most mages hit a ceiling on what
                  they can hold in mind and execute cleanly, Lexie's
                  ceiling is functionally invisible in her domain.
                  Strategic patience. She can hold a plan across a
                  long timeline and execute it at the right moment
                  without losing her grip on the pieces.
                  The faction's infrastructure exists because of her.
                  She does not just build structures — she builds
                  systems that outlast the moment they were needed for.

Flaws           : The time requirement for her peak combat output is
                  a genuine vulnerability. She knows it and has
                  developed workarounds, but in a world that moves
                  fast, this is always a tactical constraint.
                  The twin bond is a source of strength and also a
                  weight. She feels what happens to Alex in a way
                  that does not switch off. This is not discussed.

Fears           : Something happening to Alex that she absorbs
                  too late to prevent.
                  Building something magnificent and watching it
                  fail because of a factor she did not account for.

Motivations     : Build things that matter. Not just structures —
                  the faction as a functioning civilization. She
                  thinks in terms of what endures.
                  Be the person the team can rely on when the
                  foundation needs to hold.
                  Push her mastery until there is no spell she
                  cannot learn if she decides to.

Moral Alignment : Principled. She has a clear code and she holds it
                  quietly. Does not moralize. Does not need to —
                  her actions are consistent enough that her
                  values are self-evident to anyone paying attention.

Speech Style    : Deliberate. She chooses words the way she chooses
                  building materials: for load-bearing capacity,
                  not decoration. Dry humor that surfaces occasionally
                  and lands because it is not expected.

TALENT 1: FRACTAL FORM / GRAND ARCHITECT
Classification  : Craft / Technique — Mastery variant with combat application
Timing          : Composite

CORE:
  Lexie's talent operates through geometry. She perceives and interacts with
  shape and form at a fundamental level — not just physical architecture
  but the structure underlying magic, formations, and constructed systems.
  Fractal Form means every structure she engages with has a recursive
  depth she can read and work within.

POWER ROAD:
  MASTERY        The foundation. No spell is beyond her capacity to
                 learn, given sufficient study. Complexity does not
                 deter her — it is the medium she is most comfortable in.
                 Her edge over other mages is not raw power but the
                 ability to execute configurations they cannot hold.

  MAGIC CIRCLES  Her casting method. Where other mages use conventional
                 spell activation, Lexie casts through magic circle
                 construction — intricate, precise, and capable of
                 producing effects that standard casting architecture
                 cannot replicate. The circles themselves are an art form.
                 Higher complexity circles take more time but produce
                 disproportionately greater output.

  CONSTRUCTION   The non-combat expression. She is the only faction
                 member capable of reading and using System-awarded
                 Blueprints. Everything the faction builds physically
                 passes through her hands — and everything she touches
                 is enhanced by the talent's architectural affinity.
                 The faction's infrastructure is a direct product
                 of this expression.

  WAR APPLICATION In time and with preparation, Lexie can bring
                 comprehensive, devastating magical force to a
                 battlefield. Her magic circle arrays, once deployed,
                 operate as systems — coordinated outputs across
                 an area that most individual mages could not
                 replicate in scale or sustained intensity.
                 Given enough time to prepare, she is one of the most
                 dangerous forces the faction can deploy.

TALENT 2: TWIN SOUL BASTION
Classification  : Physical Enhancement / Defensive — shared talent
Timing          : Composite
Shared with     : Alex

CORE:
  The Twin Soul Bastion is not two talents — it is one talent split
  across two bodies at the moment of Ascension. Lexie and Alex each
  carry half of something that functions as a whole.

EFFECTS:
  MENTAL FORTRESS  Both twins carry tremendous natural resistance to
                   mental interference, psychic attacks, and any
                   ability that targets the mind or will. This is
                   passive and always active. The strength of the
                   resistance scales with the proximity of the other twin.

  DAMAGE TRANSFER  When one twin receives a blow that would be fatal
                   or critically debilitating, the damage can be
                   distributed between them — halving the impact across
                   both rather than concentrating it in one.
                   This is not a toggle. It is an instinctive activation
                   that occurs at threshold. The twin who receives the
                   redistributed damage knows it is coming a split second
                   before it arrives. No control over whether it triggers
                   — only awareness.

  PROXIMITY BOND   When Lexie and Alex are within range of each other,
                   both their individual abilities are enhanced. The
                   enhancement scales with the depth of their bond and
                   their combined rank. Separation diminishes this effect.
                   Extended separation begins to apply a low-level
                   drain — the talent wants them together.

  COST             The damage transfer is not clean. Distributing
                   a fatal blow means both twins take serious damage
                   rather than one taking catastrophic damage. This is
                   survivable but not without consequence. The choice
                   of whether to let a blow land on one or split it
                   is not always available — sometimes the talent
                   decides before the mind catches up.

[ CHARACTER ARC ]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Start State     : A mage of exceptional potential who has arrived with
                  her twin, found a structure worth building into,
                  and begun constructing the faction's foundation with
                  the same quiet certainty she brings to everything.

Internal Goal   : Build something that has never existed before and
                  know she built it — not as support, but as author.

External Goal   : Become the faction's permanent architectural and
                  magical infrastructure. Push Fractal Form until
                  there is no construction — physical or magical —
                  that is beyond her reach.

Turning Point   : A moment where the twin bond's damage-sharing
                  triggers under circumstances that make the cost
                  undeniable. The talent is generous and the price
                  is real. This is the beat that makes both twins
                  reckon with what they carry.

End State       : TBD — open. The Grand Architect in full expression
                  is someone who has authored entire systems. A war
                  deployment from Lexie at high rank is an event.

Theme/Lesson    : Mastery is not the same as control. To master
                  something is to understand it so well you are
                  no longer afraid of it. Control means the cost
                  stops mattering. They are not the same and Lexie
                  will have to learn the difference.
