[ PERSONALITY ]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Core Traits     : Perceptive. Steady. Privately ambitious in a way
                  she does not advertise. Exceptionally good at
                  reading people and situations. The kind of person
                  who solves a problem three steps before anyone
                  else has identified it as a problem.

Strengths       : Operational intelligence. She does not just see
                  what is happening — she sees what is going to happen
                  and moves accordingly.
                  Trustworthy in a deep sense. People give her authority
                  because she has earned it through consistent reliability,
                  not through claiming it.
                  Large-scale thinking. She manages systems, people,
                  and long timelines simultaneously without losing
                  the thread of any of them.
                  Her care for the people around her is genuine and it
                  makes her better at everything she does, not worse.

Flaws           : Her own needs tend to come last. She is so capable
                  of holding everything up that she rarely lets anyone
                  notice when she is the one carrying the weight.
                  The emotional distance in Jay that she navigates
                  so well means she never pushes him — and there are
                  moments she should.
                  She accepted it but has ambitions of her own that
                  the faction role can crowd out if she is not careful.

Fears           : The people she holds together falling apart in a
                  way she did not see coming.
                  Becoming so essential to the structure that she
                  loses the thread of who she is outside it.

Motivations     : Build something that lasts. Not power for its own
                  sake — legacy. A faction that is more than the sum
                  of its strongest fighters.
                  Protect the people she chose. This is not secondary
                  to the first motivation. They are the same thing.
                  Become something extraordinary in her own right.
                  The background role is chosen, not imposed — but
                  she has not put a ceiling on where she goes from it.

Moral Alignment : Has a clear sense of right that she does not
                  compromise easily. Will not harm without cause.
                  Will also not stand aside when something needs to
                  be done. Pragmatic at the edges, principled at
                  the center.

Speech Style    : Measured. She does not rush words. Warm without
                  being soft. Occasionally she says the precise
                  thing that cuts through a room's noise and everyone
                  goes quiet without knowing why. She is very
                  aware of the weight her words carry and uses that
                  deliberately.

[ TALENT ]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Name            : Stellar Affinity
Classification  : Elemental / Magical — Cosmic / Celestial variant
Timing          : Composite

CORE:
  Samantha carries an innate connection to stellar bodies. She does not
  just observe stars — she is in relationship with them. They are a
  power source she can draw upon, a presence she can channel, and
  eventually a force she can direct on behalf of others.

POWER ROAD:
  AFFINITY       The foundation. Stars are not hostile to her. Their
                 power flows toward her more readily than it should.
                 Passive enhancement to Mystery and Willpower in the
                 presence of open sky, stronger under stars.

  BLESSING       The first active expression. She can extend stellar
                 energy outward, granting those around her enhanced
                 attributes, resistance, and resilience. The support
                 specialization begins here. The range and potency
                 of her blessings scale with her rank and the
                 distance to the stellar body she is drawing from.

  LARGE SCALE    Her mana pool is exceptional even within the
                 Extraordinary-range mage tier. She is built for
                 spells that others cannot sustain — area effects,
                 long-duration buffs, and large-scale phenomena
                 that require more reserve than precision.

  STELLAR CALL   The late expression. Astral entities — beings native
                 to the space between stars — become available to her
                 as summons. These are not summoned monsters. They are
                 entities from a dimension adjacent to stellar bodies
                 who recognize her as a bridge. Their nature and
                 capability vary.

  STELLAR KNIGHT The most personal expression of the late talent.
                 Samantha can grant a permanent stellar mark to
                 individuals she chooses — elevating their capabilities
                 through an ongoing connection to her stellar network.
                 Stellar Knights carry a piece of her blessing as a
                 permanent state. The number she can sustain is limited
                 by her rank and reserve.

[ CHARACTER ARC ]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Start State     : A woman who has already demonstrated she can hold
                  a structure together, who has chosen a support role
                  from a position of genuine capability, and who carries
                  something unresolved with Jay that neither of them
                  has named.

Internal Goal   : Figure out who she is beyond what she provides
                  to the people around her.

External Goal   : Build the faction into something that can survive
                  without needing to be held together by hand.
                  Move from background operator to a power in her
                  own right. Become the Star Queen in fact, not
                  just in eventual title.

Turning Point   : The moment the faction faces a crisis her operational
                  skill cannot manage alone and she has to step into
                  a combat role she has been quietly building toward.

End State       : TBD — open. She becomes a node. Not just a support
                  figure but a gravitational presence — someone whose
                  network of blessings, knights, and stellar entities
                  makes her a strategic force no one planned around.

Theme/Lesson    : Choosing to support is not the same as choosing to
                  be secondary. The person who builds the foundation
                  does not disappear into it. At some point the
                  foundation speaks.
